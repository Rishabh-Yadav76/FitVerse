Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our gyms and services

What are your membership options?

We offer flexible membership options including monthly, quarterly, and annual plans. We also have special packages for students, seniors, and corporate clients. Visit our Services page or contact us for detailed pricing information.

Do you offer personal training services?

Yes, we have certified personal trainers available at all our locations. You can choose from one-on-one sessions or small group training. Our trainers will work with you to create a customized fitness plan based on your goals and fitness level.

What safety measures are in place?

Your safety is our priority. All our gyms follow strict hygiene protocols, including regular sanitization of equipment, social distancing measures, and mandatory temperature checks. Our staff is fully vaccinated and trained in COVID-19 safety procedures.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, we understand that circumstances change. You can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year with proper documentation (medical certificate, travel proof, etc.). Please contact our support team to initiate a freeze.

Do you have women-only sections?

Most of our locations have dedicated women-only workout areas with separate changing rooms. Please check with your specific location for details about their women's section facilities.
